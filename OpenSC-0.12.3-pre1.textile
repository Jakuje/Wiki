h1. OpenSC 0.12.3-pre1

Download <b>NightlyBuilds</b> for testing most recent changes, what will eventually be the next release candidate. When a release candidate is created (from "https://www.opensc-project.org/autobuild/view/Staging/),":https://www.opensc-project.org/autobuild/view/Staging/), direct download links are available on this page.

PreReleases are built from the [source:@staging staging branch] ("Github":https://github.com/OpenSC/OpenSC/tree/staging, "gitweb":https://www.opensc-project.org/codereview/gitweb?p=OpenSC.git;a=tree;h=refs/heads/staging;hb=refs/heads/staging) of the SourceCode repository. Builds are available for download from "http://www.opensc-project.org/downloads/nightly/staging/":http://www.opensc-project.org/downloads/nightly/staging/

Please test and post your feedback to "opensc-devel mailing list":wiki/Mailing-lists.
